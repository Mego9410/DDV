01

The problem

You run a practice, not a procurement department

Every pound you overpay a supplier comes straight off your profit — and straight off your practice value when you come to sell. Suppliers know exactly what the market charges. Most principals don't.

  1. 01

    Renewals roll over on autopilot

    Insurance, energy, telecoms, waste contracts — they renew quietly every year, often with an uplift, because nobody has time to go back out to the market. Inertia is the most expensive supplier you have.

  2. 02

    Suppliers price for the busy

    Materials and lab pricing drifts up in small increments that never justify a confrontation on their own. Across a year, across every line, the drift adds up to tens of thousands — invisible unless someone audits invoice by invoice.

  3. 03

    Tendering properly is a full-time job

    Getting genuinely competitive quotes means knowing the market, the suppliers, the benchmarks and the negotiating levers in each category. Between surgeries, staff and CQC, that job never reaches the top of your list. So it never happens.

The deal

No savings, no fee. At all.

There is no upfront cost, no retainer, and no invoice unless we deliver. Our specialists review 12 months of your supplier invoices, benchmark every line against what the market actually charges, and only get paid out of the savings they find. If we find nothing, the review has cost you nothing — and you'll know your costs are sharp.

And you keep control. Around 60% of practices stay with their existing suppliers on better terms — we improve the price and the service, not necessarily the relationship. You approve every change before it happens. Nothing moves without your sign-off.

The only thing you risk by applying is finding out you're already paying the right price.

06

Questions

Asked by every principal, answered straight

What does it actually cost?

Nothing upfront, nothing ongoing, and nothing at all if no savings are found. Our fee is a share of the savings we deliver — verified against your invoices — so we are only paid out of money you weren't keeping anyway. Founding practices get the best share terms we will ever offer.

Will I have to change suppliers I trust?

No. Around 60% of practices stay with their existing suppliers on improved pricing and terms. Every recommendation — stay or switch — is yours to approve or decline. Nothing changes without your sign-off.

How much of my time will it take?

A review call, access to 12 months of supplier invoices (your practice manager or accountant can provide these), and sign-off on the recommendations. The analysis, tendering, negotiation and 24-month monitoring are all done for you.

Is my data safe?

Your invoices and figures are used solely to run your review, held confidentially, and never shared or published in a way that identifies your practice — the same standard behind every Dental Data Vault benchmark.

Why is the cohort limited to 25?

Because a forensic review is specialist-intensive and we won't dilute it at launch. When the 25 places are taken, applications stay open for the general waiting list — but founding terms go with the founding places.

Does applying commit me to anything?

No. Applying reserves your position in the queue. A place is only confirmed after your review call, and terms are agreed in writing before any work begins.
